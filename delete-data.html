How to Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete your account data at any time. To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the app.
  2. Log in with your Facebook account.
  3. Go to the Settings menu (or Profile section).
  4. Select the option "Delete Account" or "Delete My Data".
  5. Confirm your choice. All saved data will be permanently removed from our servers.

If you're unable to delete your data through the app, you can contact us directly through the GitHub Issues page of the project. Include your user ID or email used in the app.
